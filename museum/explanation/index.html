Memento

Memento Structure: Two intercutting timelines: a color one moving backward in time and a black-and-white one moving forward.

Inception

Inception Structure: Multiple layers of dreams.

Dunkirk

Three parallel timelines, each with a different duration: one for the soldiers on the beach (one week), one for the navy on the sea (one day), and one for the pilots in the air (one hour).

Tenet

The first half of the film moves forward, and the second half moves backward in time
